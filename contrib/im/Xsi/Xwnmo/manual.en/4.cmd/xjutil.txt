NAME
     xjutil - Dictionary Utirity Manager

SYNOPSIS
     /usr/bin/xjutil _d_i_s_p_l_a_y-_n_a_m_e _a_t_o_m-_n_a_m_e _a_c_o_u_n_t

DESCRIPTION
     The _x_j_u_t_i_l is a dictionary utility manager operates dic-
     tionaries of _W_n_n. It is executed by _x_w_n_m_o automatically.

OPARATIONS
     _X_j_u_t_i_l has the following operations for dictionary of _W_n_n.

     Add dictionary:
             You can add new dictionary in your conversion
             environment.

     Output list of dictionaries:
             You can see a list of current loaded dictionaries in
             your conversion environment.

     Registry:
             You can registry new word in your private diction-
             ary.

     Search: You can search word in dictionary. And if you push
             any button on one word , you can operate its word.
             (_d_e_t_e_l_e/_d_i_s_a_b_l_e/_e_n_a_b_l_e/_c_o_m_m_e_n_t)

     Change parameters:
             You can change some parameter of conversion environ-
             ment.

     Output information of dictionaries:
             You can see feather infomations of dictionaries. And
             if you push any button on one dictionary, you can
             operate its dictionary.
             (_d_e_t_e_l_e/_d_i_s_a_b_l_e/_e_n_a_b_l_e/_c_o_m_m_e_n_t)

     Change Accessory Vocabulary:
             You can change a accessory vocabulary file.
